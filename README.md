📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

📌 Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover key insights into product performance, regional profitability, and overall business trends.
The goal was to transform raw data into an interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making.

---

🧰 Tools Used

- Tableau (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)

---

📁 Dataset

- Superstore Sales Dataset
- Includes data on sales, profit, categories, regions, and customer orders

🔍 Key Business Insights

- Technology drives the highest revenue but does not have the highest profit margin
- Office Supplies generates lower revenue but maintains consistent profitability
- Eastern Asia is a key profit driver, indicating strong regional performance
- Southern Asia and Oceania also contribute significantly to overall profit
- Some regions show lower profitability, suggesting inefficiencies or untapped potential

---

💡 Recommendations

- Increase marketing spend on Technology products to maximize revenue potential
- Optimize pricing and cost strategies for Office Supplies to maintain profitability
- Expand operations and investment in high-performing regions like Eastern Asia
- Investigate underperforming regions to identify opportunities for improvement
